Show Notes: DJ set by Ambrose, Cavs, Cook and Joey. BUSSIT (Joey Valence & Brae) was played very briefly and was possibly accidental. Ambrose jumped on a chair and was held up by Cavs and Joey during Dog Dribble (Getdown Services). Cavs rode on Joey's shoulders during U Should Not Be Doing That (Amyl and the Sniffers). Cavs poured beer into the mouths of the front row during Dragula (Rob Zombie). A song was briefly played though it seemed to be an accident as Joey explained that they were having technical difficulties and asked for requests. Joey imitated Brian Johnson during Thunderstruck (AC/DC). Afterwards he asked the audience for requests which included Never Gonna Give You Up by Rick Astley and anything by George Michael, Nine Inch Nails, James Brown, Bill Withers and Green Day. During Rush (Troye Sivan) Joey said that Troye Sivan was supposed to show up to King Gizzard's show on 2025-05-23. Joey said "Free Palestine" during Basha (Hassan Abou Alam). Ambrose said he "fucked up" You Know How We Do It (Ice Cube) and chose You Can Do It (Ice Cube) instead. Before 1 Thing (Amerie) Ambrose said that it was his favorite song of all time and that it has the best drum sound ever. Ambrose introduced Thong Song (Sisqo) by explaining the Australian meaning of thong (flip-flop) and said that it always confused him. Ambrose said that Toxic (Britney Spears) was the best song ever, said "Free Britney" and then said he wished he was as badass as her. Before Party All the Time (Eddie Murphy) Ambrose introduced Cook to the stage and called him his best friend, which Cook agreed with. Ambrose also said that its Cook's favorite song.

Opening Band(s): Xtrarradio DJs, David Undersounds DJ
